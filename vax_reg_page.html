Get Vaccinated against COVID-19

Register online, by phone, or with BC Service Offices.

Register Online

To register online, you must provide:
  • First and last name
  • Date of birth
  • Postal Code
  • Personal Health Number (PHN)
  • Email address or phone number

Register by Phone

If you don't have a Personal Health Number (PHN), you can register by phone. A PHN will be created for you.
We are available seven days a week, 7am-7pm (PDT).
Call: 1-833-838-2323 (Translators available)
Call: 1-604-681-4261 (Outside Canada/USA)
Dial: 7-11 (Telephone for Deaf)

Register at a BC Service Office

You can register in-person at all Service BC offices.
